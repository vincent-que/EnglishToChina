GAMAK IndustriesFiscal Year 2024Published: January 2025

Chapter 1: Executive Summary

This document presents the annual financial and operational performance of GAMAK Industries for the fiscal year ending December 31, 2024. The company achieved record revenue growth driven by expansion into emerging markets and the successful launch of three new product lines.

[2]

Chapter 2: Financial Overview

Total revenue for FY2024 reached $2.84 billion, representing a 15.3% increase from the prior year. Operating margins improved to 18.7%, up from 16.2% in FY2023, reflecting successful cost optimization initiatives and improved operational efficiency across manufacturing facilities.

Key Financial Metrics

第一章 执行摘要

本文档呈现了 GAMAK 工业集团截至 2024 年 12 月 31 日财年的财务与运营表现。公司实现了创纪录的收入增长，这得益于向新兴市场的拓展以及三条新产品线的成功推出。
